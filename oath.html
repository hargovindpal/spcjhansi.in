Oath of the Nursing Student

  • I solemnly pledge myself to consecrate my life to the service of humanity. I will practice my profession with integrity and compassion. I will respect and uphold the dignity of my profession and the rights of those I serve..

  • I will strive for excellence in my knowledge and skills, and I will contribute to the advancement of my field. I will adhere to the ethical standards of nursing and act with honesty and responsibility.

  • In all circumstances, I will maintain the highest level of professional conduct and advocate for the well-being of my patients. I take this oath freely and upon my honor.
