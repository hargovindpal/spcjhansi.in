X-Ray Tech

Course Details:

  • Course Duration: 2 Years
  • Mode of Study: : Full-Time
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)

Why Study X-Ray Tech?

  • High Demand: The need for skilled radiologic technologists is growing, offering ample job opportunities in hospitals, clinics, diagnostic imaging centers, and more.
  • Impactful Work: Play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating patients by providing essential imaging services.
  • Technological Advancements: Work with cutting-edge imaging technology and stay updated with advancements in the field.
  • Career Growth: Opportunities for specialization and advancement into roles such as MRI Technologist, CT Technologist, or Radiology Manager.

Course Overview:

The X-Ray Tech program at Saroj Paramedical Degree College is designed to provide students with comprehensive knowledge and hands-on experience in diagnostic imaging. This program trains students to operate X-ray machines, perform diagnostic imaging procedures, and work effectively in various medical settings.


Key Learning Areas:

  • Introduction to Radiology: Understanding the fundamentals of radiology, including the types and uses of diagnostic imaging techniques.
  • X-Ray Physics and Equipment: Study of X-ray machines, their operation, and maintenance.
  • Radiographic Techniques: Training in different X-ray imaging techniques and positioning to obtain accurate diagnostic images.
  • Radiation Protection: Learning about radiation safety, protection measures for patients and healthcare workers, and regulatory standards.
  • Patient Care and Communication: Skills for interacting with patients, preparing them for procedures, and ensuring their comfort and safety.
  • Image Analysis and Interpretation: Basic training in analyzing X-ray images and recognizing common abnormalities.
  • Clinical Practice: Hands-on experience in a clinical setting, working under supervision to perform X-ray procedures and interact with patients.

Career Opportunities:

Graduates of the X-Ray Tech program can pursue careers as:

  • X-Ray Technicians
  • Radiologic Technologists
  • Medical Imaging Technicians
  • Radiology Assistants
  • Imaging Equipment Sales Representatives
