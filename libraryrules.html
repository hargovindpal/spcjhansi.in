LIBRARY RULES

1. Respect for Library Property:

  • Handle all library materials with care and return them in the same condition.

2. Borrowing Limits:

  • Follow the specified limits for the number of items you can borrow.

3. Return Deadlines:

  • Adhere to due dates to avoid late fees and ensure availability for others.

4. Lost or Damaged Items:

  • Report lost or damaged materials immediately and be prepared to pay replacement costs.

5. Quiet Environment:

  • Maintain silence and avoid disruptions to provide a conducive study environment.

6. Library Access:

  • Only authorized students and staff are permitted to use library facilities.

7. Food and Drink:

  • No food or drinks allowed in the library to protect resources.

By following these rules, we ensure that our library environment remains conducive to learning and professional development.
Thank you for your cooperation!
