General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM)

  • Duration: 3 Years with 6 Months of Mandatory Internship.
  • Eligibility: 10+2 in Science stream with an average aggregate score of 50%

Why Study GNM Nursing Courses?

  • The GNM nursing course is crucial for those who aspire to make a difference in the healthcare sector. The curriculum covers comprehensive patient care techniques, midwifery, and nursing practices. Students gain hands-on experience through mandatory internships, which helps in building practical skills and confidence.

Course Overview:

The General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) course at Mangal Maitri Paramedical College is designed to equip students with the skills necessary to provide high-quality patient care. The program emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing graduates to work effectively in various healthcare environments.


Top Recruiters:

  • Private Hospitals: Max Hospitals, Apollo Hospitals, Fortis Hospitals, Manipal Hospitals
  • Government Hospitals: AIIMS Delhi, IPGMER Kolkata


Average Salary (INR): 3.50 LPA – 5 LPA



Career Opportunities:

  • Graduates can work in various settings, including hospitals, clinics, community health centers, and nursing homes. They can also pursue further education and specialization in nursing.

Course Highlights:

  • Comprehensive nursing and midwifery training
  • Hands-on practical experience through internships
  • Opportunities to work with top private and government hospitals
  • Preparation for a rewarding career in healthcare
