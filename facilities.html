Facilities At Saroj Paramedical Degree College, Jhansi

At Saroj Paramedical Degree College, we are dedicated to providing our students with a comprehensive learning experience. Our state-of-the-art facilities ensure that every student has the resources and environment needed to excel in their paramedical education.

AC AUDITORIUM

At Saroj Paramedical & Degree College, we are committed to providing our students with an environment that fosters learning, growth, and development. Our state-of-the-art facilities ensure that our students have everything they need to succeed in their academic and extracurricular endeavors.

CENTRAL LIBRARY

Our well-stocked library offers a vast collection of textbooks, reference materials, and research journals essential for paramedical studies. The serene and conducive environment supports focused study and research, helping students stay ahead in their academic pursuits.

CAFETERIA

The cafeteria serves a variety of nutritious and delicious meals, catering to diverse tastes and dietary needs. It's a place where students can relax, socialize, and recharge between classes.

SPORTS

Our spacious ground is ideal for physical activities, sports, and recreational events. It provides a venue for students to engage in healthy activities and develop teamwork and leadership skills.

TRANSPORT FACILITIES

We offer reliable transportation services to facilitate easy commuting for students and staff. Our fleet of well-maintained vehicles ensures timely and safe travel to and from the college.

INTERNET & WIFI-CAMPUS

Stay connected with our high-speed internet and free Wi-Fi across the campus. Whether you're researching for assignments or collaborating on projects, our reliable network ensures you have the connectivity you need.

HOSPITAL

Our college is closely affiliated with a reputed hospital, providing students with hands-on clinical training and exposure to real-world medical environments. This association enhances practical learning and skill development.

HOSTEL FACILITY

Our comfortable and secure hostel accommodations provide a home away from home. With well-furnished rooms, nutritious meals, and dedicated staff, students can enjoy a balanced and supportive living environment.
