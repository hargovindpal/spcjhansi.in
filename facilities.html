AC AUDITORIUM

At Mangal Maitri Paramedical College, we are committed to providing our students with an environment that fosters learning, growth, and development. Our state-of-the-art facilities ensure that our students have everything they need to succeed in their academic and extracurricular endeavors.

CENTRAL LIBRARY

Our well-equipped library offers a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources, providing students with access to a wealth of knowledge across various fields of medical science. It's a quiet and conducive environment for study and research.

HYGIENIC ENVIRONMENT

MEDICAL FACILITIES

Our on-campus hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology and serves as a practical training ground for our students. Under the supervision of experienced professionals, students gain hands-on experience that prepares them for real-world medical scenarios.

SPORTS

We believe in the importance of physical well-being and offer a well-maintained sports ground for various outdoor activities. Whether it's for organized sports or recreational use, our ground provides ample space for students to engage in physical exercise and team-building activities.

TRANSPORT FACILITIES

Our college provides reliable transportation facilities to ensure that students and staff can commute easily to and from the campus. Our fleet of buses covers various routes across the city for the convenience of our students.

WIFI-CAMPUS

Our entire campus is equipped with high-speed internet and free Wi-Fi access, allowing students to stay connected, conduct research, and collaborate with peers online.

HOSPITAL

HOSTEL FACILITY

We offer comfortable and secure hostel accommodations for students from out of town. Our hostels are designed to provide a home-like atmosphere, ensuring a safe and nurturing environment for all residents.
