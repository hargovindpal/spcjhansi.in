About Institution

Welcome To BHALCHANDRA Group of Institutions

  • The institute has been awarded ‘A’ Grade by UGC-NAAC.
  • In 2003, the group was the first to establish a private B.Ed. college in the Lucknow region.
  • In 2010, the group was the first to establish a private D.EL.Ed. (BTC) college in the Lucknow region.

BHALCHANDRA Group of Institutions, Lucknow established in the year 2002 carries a legacy of more than 20 years of Excellence in the field of Quality Education. It has got the prestigious ‘A’ Grade Accreditation by NAAC. It was the First educational institution in Lucknow to be awarded ‘A’ Grade by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), an autonomous body of the University Grants Commission (UGC) in the year 2011, which is an honour very few colleges enjoy. It was also the First to introduce the Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) course in Lucknow in the year 2002 with the establishment of the First private teacher education college in the city of Lucknow. It is Recognised by NCTE, INC, PCI, AICTE and Affiliated to Lucknow University. One of the most sought-after colleges by the students of Northern India, Bhalchandra Group of Institutions offers B.Ed, B.P.Ed, D.EL.Ed (BTC), B.EL.Ed, BSc Nursing, ANM, GNM, BSc Agriculture, D.Pharma, BBA, PGDM and DHP courses.
