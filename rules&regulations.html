Rules & Regulations

At Saroj Paramedical Degree College, we are dedicated to fostering a learning environment that upholds the highest standards of academic excellence, professionalism, and ethical conduct. Our policies are designed to ensure that all students, faculty, and staff contribute to and benefit from a supportive and productive educational atmosphere.

Attendance

  • Regular attendance is mandatory for all students. Absences must be justified with valid reasons and documented through proper channels.
  • Students must adhere to the attendance policy specified for each course, as excessive absenteeism may affect their academic standing and progression.

Discipline

  • Students are expected to demonstrate professional behavior both on campus and during clinical placements. Any form of misconduct, including but not limited to bullying, harassment, or dishonesty, will be addressed with appropriate disciplinary actions.
  • Respect for faculty, staff, and fellow students is paramount. Disruptive or disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.

Academic Integrity

  • Plagiarism, cheating, and other forms of academic dishonesty are strictly prohibited. Students must adhere to the academic integrity guidelines and complete all assessments independently unless collaboration is explicitly permitted.
  • Any breach of academic integrity will result in serious consequences, including possible expulsion.

Dress Code

  • Students are required to adhere to the prescribed dress code, including uniforms and hygiene standards, as specified by the college and respective clinical placements.
  • Proper personal grooming and professional appearance are expected at all times.

Health and Safety

  • Students must follow all health and safety protocols to ensure a safe learning environment. This includes compliance with infection control practices and reporting any health concerns to the appropriate authorities.
  • Emergency procedures and safety drills must be attended and followed diligently.

Use of Facilities

  • College facilities and resources should be used responsibly and only for their intended purposes. Any damage or misuse of property will be subject to penalties.
  • Personal belongings should be secured, and the college is not responsible for lost or stolen items.

Communication

  • All official communications will be conducted through college email or other designated platforms. Students are responsible for regularly checking these channels for updates and announcements.
  • Any grievances or concerns should be reported through the established channels, and the college is committed to addressing them in a fair and timely manner.

Code of Conduct

  • Students are expected to maintain a high standard of ethical and professional behavior. This includes respecting confidentiality, demonstrating empathy in clinical settings, and adhering to all college policies.
  • Violation of the code of conduct may result in disciplinary actions ranging from warnings to expulsion, depending on the severity of the infraction.

Note: For further details on specific policies or to address any concerns, students are encouraged to contact the administration office.
