Trustee's Message

Dear Students, Parents, and Esteemed Colleagues

Welcome to Mangal Maitri Paramedical College, where we are dedicated to nurturing the next generation of healthcare professionals. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills, ensuring that our students are well-prepared to meet the challenges of the medical field.
At Mangal Maitri, we offer a range of courses, including General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM), Operation Theatre Technology (OT-Tec), and Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT). Each program is meticulously designed to equip our students with the expertise and hands-on experience needed to excel in their respective fields.
We believe in fostering an environment where students can thrive, supported by a team of experienced faculty and state-of-the-art facilities. Our commitment to excellence drives us to continually enhance our curriculum and provide the best possible training for our students.
Thank you for considering Mangal Maitri Paramedical College as your educational partner. Together, we are building tomorrow’s medical leaders.

"In every student lies the promise of a healthier future."

Dr.Satya Narayan Kanchan
